about me

About

I'm Dani and I'm located in the San Francisco Bay Area. I work as an instructor, curriculum developer, and produdct manager for Podium Education, delivering tech-empowered courses to undergrad students across the globe. I'm a former senior instructor for Actualize Coding Bootcamp, and have helped hundreds of students transition into tech careers.

I attended UC Santa Cruz where I graduated with a bachelors of science in biology. While pursuing a career in my field, I worked with talented software engineers and became interested in programming, so I enrolled in an immersive web-development bootcamp. I fell in love with coding and design and while I worked as an industry engineer and designer for a while, I realized my home was in the bootcamp world teaching the next generation of developers.
